## 🔬 Quantitative Research & Working Papers (GitHub Spotlight)

*This profile hosts the core implementation, historical backtesting engines, and vectorised math environments validating my research.*

### 🔍 [Volatility Targeting, Variance Drain, and the Financialization Effect](https://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.6575821)
* **Mathematical Framework:** Derived a closed-form Sharpe improvement from a unified Heston–Merton framework, formally linking volatility targeting to Merton-optimal allocation and variance drain reduction.
* **Empirical Validation:** Built a high-performance backtesting engine to validate the framework across **100 years of CRSP data** (21 assets, 14 international markets).
* **The Alpha:** Proposed the **VDOV rule**, achieving an out-of-sample Sharpe ratio of **1.01** vs. the 0.94 Moreira & Muir benchmark (0.79 buy-and-hold).

### 📐 [Factor Exposure and Correlation Decomposition](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6575821)
* **The Problem:** Developed the **Diversification Quality Index (DQI)** to uncover hidden risk concentration in seemingly diversified multi-asset portfolios.
* **The Implementation:** Modeled correlation spikes during market stress using close to two decades of data across 25 assets. The tactical DQI strategy cut maximum drawdown nearly in half compared to equal-weight benchmarks.

### 🤖 Delta-Guided Soft Actor-Critic for Derivative Hedging
* **Framework:** Proposed a novel reinforcement learning reward-shaping scheme embedding Black–Scholes delta as a time-decaying inductive bias with an original asymptotic optimality proof.
* **Performance:** Reduced hedging error by **19–38%** across GBM, Merton jump-diffusion, and rough Bergomi ($H=0.07$) environments with a $2\times$ convergence speedup over vanilla SAC.
